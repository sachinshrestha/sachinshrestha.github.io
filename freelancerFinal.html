Problem: Use an individuals information to predict whether or not the person earns more than $50,000 per year.

Data: Census data for earnings, 2010

For the purpose of solving this problem, I have built a LOGISTIC REGRESSION MODEL, a CART MODEL, a RANDOM FOREST MODEL and a CART MODEL WITH CROSS-VALIDATION and shown their accuracies. The accuracies may help choose the best model.

I wrote this script over the weekend. In the availability of more time, I would refine the models and recommend the best model for the purpose of this problem.

I hope that my script serves well for an initial evaluation.

Thank you.
